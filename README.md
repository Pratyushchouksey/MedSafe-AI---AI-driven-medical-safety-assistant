# MedSafe-AI---AI-driven-medical-safety-assistant
🩺 MedSafe AI  MedSafe AI is an AI-powered healthcare assistance platform designed to improve medication safety awareness, symptom understanding, and early health risk identification through a unified web-based system.
Built with Streamlit, OCR, fuzzy matching algorithms, curated medical datasets, and Large Language Models (LLMs), the platform helps users better understand medicines and health symptoms in an educational, non-diagnostic manner.

The system integrates multiple safety-focused healthcare tools into one interface, allowing users to analyze prescriptions, check medicine interactions, monitor side effects, and receive basic symptom guidance. By combining computer vision, natural language processing, fuzzy logic, and generative AI, MedSafe AI provides structured and user-friendly safety insights to support informed health decisions.

🚀 Key Features
💊 Medicine Interaction Checker

Enter multiple medicines at once

Uses fuzzy matching to recognize medicine names

Detects potential drug–drug interactions

Provides simplified AI-generated safety explanations

📄 Prescription Analyzer

Upload images of handwritten prescriptions

Uses OCR + AI to extract medicine names and active salts

Outputs structured information for easier understanding

🤒 Symptom & Doubt Solver

Describe symptoms in natural language

Receive educational guidance and precautionary advice

Includes lifestyle tips, diet suggestions, and warning signs

📊 Side-Effect Monitor

Log post-medication experiences

AI analyzes possible contributing factors

Provides clear safety suggestions for monitoring symptoms

🚨 Emergency Risk Predictor

Evaluates symptoms using rule-based risk scoring

Assigns a transparent risk level

Suggests appropriate next steps when urgent care may be needed

🛠️ Tech Stack

Frontend / Interface: Streamlit

Language: Python

OCR: Tesseract / Computer Vision

Medicine Matching: Fuzzy Matching Algorithms

AI & NLP: Large Language Models (LLMs)

Data: Curated medical and drug databases

🎯 Purpose

MedSafe AI aims to improve medication literacy, preventive healthcare awareness, and early risk detection. It provides accessible tools for patients, caregivers, and health-tech researchers to better understand medicines, symptoms, and potential safety risks.

⚠️ Disclaimer:
MedSafe AI provides educational and informational guidance only. It does not provide medical diagnoses or replace professional medical consultation
